About Me

I'm a middle school math teacher and a married mother of two kids under 5.
I'm also a runner.
Through the majority of my 40 years, running has been the one thing that I could count on to help me keep it all together.

After qualifying at the Illinois Marathon last May, I'm currently gearing up for my first trip to Boston in 2017!
