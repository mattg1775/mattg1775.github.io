Work Experience

Kerry received her undergraduate degree in History from The University of Illinois, and following graduation earned her MSEd from Loyola University, Chicago.

In 2003, Kerry was began teaching at Tilden Academy in Chicago, assigned to the Achievement Academy

After nine years of teaching within Chicago Public Schools, Kerry began at her current position at Haines Middle School in St. Charles, IL.

Since 2013, Kerr has taught all three levels of 8th Grade math: regular, advanced, and double advanced students, preparing them for a high school level curriculum.

Running

A standout on the first girls cross country team at Stagg High School, Kerry qualified for multiple state championship races.

A veteran of three previous marathons, most recently, she ran a 3:37(PR) marathon at the Illinois Marathon in April 2016. This was good enough to qualify her for entry into the Boston Marathon in April 2017.
