Summary

Matthew received his undergraduate degree from Penn State University, and following graduation was commissioned as an officer in the United States Marine Corps.

In 2003, Matt was winged a Naval Aviator and trained to fly the Sikorsky CH-53E helicopter, the Marine Corps' premier heavy-lift helicopter.

After three deployments in support of global combat and humanitarian assistance operations, Matt completed a successful recruiting tour in Wisconsin before receiving his honorable discharge in 2011.

Since that time, Matt has worked in the industrial automation technology industry in positions ranging from Sales to Program Management. He currently lives in Geneva, IL with his wife, Kerry and three children.
